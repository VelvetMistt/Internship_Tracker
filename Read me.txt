📌 Overview

InternPilot AI is a modern web application designed to help students efficiently manage their internship application journey.

Students often apply to multiple companies and struggle to keep track of application deadlines, interview schedules, recruiter communications, assessments, offers, and rejections.

InternPilot AI provides a centralized platform that enables students to organize every stage of the internship process through an intuitive dashboard, intelligent reminders, analytics, and a streamlined application management system.

The project follows a scalable software architecture and is built using modern full-stack technologies to simulate a real-world production application.

🎯 Problem Statement

Managing multiple internship applications manually is difficult.

Students commonly face challenges such as:

Missing application deadlines
Forgetting interview schedules
Losing recruiter communication
Poor organization of internship records
Difficulty tracking application status
No centralized internship management system

These problems often lead to missed opportunities and inefficient internship planning.

💡 Solution

InternPilot AI provides a centralized internship management platform where students can:

Organize internship applications
Track application progress
Schedule interviews
Receive reminders
Store recruiter information
Monitor deadlines
Visualize application analytics
Manage their internship journey efficiently
